    Disease concepts include:

    1. Disease – a particular abnormal, pathological condition that affects part or all of an organism
    2. Disease Class – a specific category of similar but different diseases
    3. Symptom – the physical manifestations of a disease
    4. Signs/diagnostic signs – symptoms or test results that support a disease diagnosis. These generally exclude genetic tests, which often define (as opposed to support) a disease diagnosis.


      5. When in doubt, search online resources like: diseasesdatabase.com and ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/mesh/ or visit our new Wikia forum for help.
